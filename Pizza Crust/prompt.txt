George has bought a pizza. George loves cheese. George thinks the pizza does not have enough cheese. George gets angry.

George’s pizza is round, and has a radius of 𝑅 cm. The outermost 𝐶 cm is crust, and does not have cheese. What percent of George’s pizza has cheese?

Input

The input consists of a single line with two space separated integers, 𝑅 and 𝐶.

Output

Output the percentage of the pizza that has cheese. Your answer must have an absolute or relative error of at most 10^−6.

Limits

1 ≤ 𝐶 ≤ 𝑅 ≤ 100

Sample Input 1               Sample Output 1
1 1                          0.000000000

Sample Input 2               Sample Output 2
2 1                          25.000000
